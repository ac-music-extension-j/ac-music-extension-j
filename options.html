About

Animal Crossing Music (J variant)

Play Animal Crossing's smooth background music in real-time throughout your day.

Contributors

The J variant was made by JBMagination (and these other lovelies) after over 3 years of no upstream maintenance, and a strong love for Animal Crossing. It is not associated with upstream in any way.

Extension originally created by Andrex (1.0), blazescorch2 (2.0), and PikaDude (alongside many others) (3.0 and 4.0). Check out their successor to this extension, AC Music App!

Idea for extension taken from Brian Lee's Animal Crossing music website.

  • Added Firefox support
  • Merged other experimental branches that never made it to the upstream development branch
